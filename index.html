What is the Closest Match?

Once your results are generated, our test will try to assign you with the economic system that is the closest to your scores. We invite you to search more about that system, to see if it seems to fit your beliefs.

If not, let us know by joining our Discord server or our subreddit and propose any features or economics you would like to see added. EconValues is meant to be built and developed collectively, so any propositions are welcomed.
